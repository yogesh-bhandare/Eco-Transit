Personal Rapid Transit Planning Services

PRT Planning for Success

Eco Transit believes that consideration of personal rapid transit should commence as early as possible in the overall project formulation stage. Only by doing this will PRT become truly integrated into the project, allowing it to provide improved transportation, as well as to potentially enhance other aspects, such as walkability, land values and reduction in congestion and parking needs.

Our personal rapid transit planning services include:

  • Community participation
  • Feasibility studies
  • Environmental assessment
  • Alternatives analysis
  • Benefit/cost analysis
  • Technology assessments
  • Ridership demand
  • Operations analysis
  • Simulation modeling
  • Facility planning
  • Renderings and animations
  • Capital and O&M cost estimates
  • Master planning
  • Procurement strategies

Eco Transit

Eco Transit Inc. is committed to providing personalized, responsive service resulting in smoothly executed personal rapid transit projects. Our business focuses on consulting services for personal rapid transit (PRT). PRT can be described as a system of driver-less taxicabs that takes passengers to their destination along dedicated guideways without intermediate stops. PRT is also known as automated transit networks (ATN) or Podcars.

Personal Rapid Transit can save time, money and space while enhancing security. Browse this website to find out more. Contact us to learn how PRT could be applied to meet your needs.
