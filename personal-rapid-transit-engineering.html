PRT Engineering Services

Professional Personal Rapid Transit Engineering Services

Eco Transit's multi-disciplinary staff and expert consultants are qualified to address all the technical and system-related issues that can arise during PRT system design procurement and implementation. Our many years of transportation and civil project experience, coupled with our thorough understanding of PRT concepts, allow us to quickly determine the strengths and weaknesses of specific PRT applications. We have a proven ability to find solutions to problems that others have believed to be intractable.

Our personal rapid transit engineering services include:

  • Conceptual design
  • Preliminary design (guideway and station layout, routing and geometrics)
  • Constructability reviews
  • Vehicle design
  • Control/communications systems
  • Safety analysis and certification
  • Value engineering
  • Incident investigation
  • Reliability, availability & maintainability (RAM) analysis
  • System integration

Members of our team have worked for equipment and system suppliers, public transit agencies, local authorities and system owners. We understand automated transit projects from many different points of view.

Eco Transit

Eco Transit is committed to providing personalized, responsive service resulting in smoothly executed personal rapid transit projects. Our business focuses on consulting services for personal rapid transit (PRT). PRT can be described as a system of driver-less taxicabs that takes passengers to their destination along dedicated guideways without intermediate stops. PRT is also known as automated transit networks (ATN) or Podcars.

Personal Rapid Transit can save time, money and space while enhancing security. Browse this website to find out more. Contact us to learn how PRT could be applied to meet your needs.
